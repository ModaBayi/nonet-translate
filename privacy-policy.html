Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

NoNet Translate is an offline AI-powered translation app. This Privacy Policy explains how NoNet Translate handles information when you use the app.

1. Offline translation

NoNet Translate is designed to process translation tasks locally on your device after the required AI model is downloaded.

Your translation text, voice input and images are not sent to our own translation server for translation processing.

2. Internet access

The app may use internet access for the following purposes:

  • Downloading AI models
  • Downloading speech or voice packages
  • Processing optional support packages through Google Play Billing

Internet access is not required for normal offline translation after the required local model and packages are available on the device.

3. Camera permission

The camera permission is used only for camera and image translation features. The app may use the camera to capture an image so that text or visual content can be processed locally for translation.

Camera input is not sent to our own server for translation processing.

4. Microphone permission

The microphone permission is used for voice translation and advanced speech recording features. The app may record your voice to convert speech into text and translate it locally on your device.

Voice input is not sent to our own server for translation processing.

5. Purchases

NoNet Translate offers optional one-time support packages through Google Play Billing. Purchases are processed by Google Play.

We do not collect or process your payment card details.

Support packages are optional. The app remains usable without a purchase. Support packages may remove support reminders inside the app.

6. Data stored on your device

The app may store some information locally on your device, such as:

  • App language
  • Selected source and target languages
  • Selected AI model
  • Supporter or purchase status
  • Local settings and preferences
  • Downloaded AI models and speech packages
  • Some local history or app state information

This information is stored on your device to make the app work properly.

7. Ads and analytics

NoNet Translate does not include advertising.

NoNet Translate does not use advertising SDKs.

NoNet Translate does not use Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics.

8. AI translation accuracy

Translations are generated by artificial intelligence and may contain mistakes. Do not rely on AI translation output as the only source for legal, medical, financial or safety-critical decisions.

9. Data deletion

You can delete locally stored app data from your device settings by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.

Downloaded models and packages may also be removed from the device if the app provides such an option or by clearing app data.
